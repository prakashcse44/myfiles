About

The ideal engineer is a composite… He is not a scientist, he is not a mathematician, he is not a sociologist or a writer, but he may use the knowledge and techniques of any or all of these disciplines in solving engineering problems


Computer Engineering is one of the most sighed disciplines of engineering courses and is the practical and scientific approach to its application. In Computer Engineering the student will go through the algorithms, programming languages, operating systems, database management systems, computer network, computer graphics and artificial intelligence.


The Institute recognizes the key role that computers play in teaching as well as research. Govt. Polytechnic Ambota is committed to providing computing facilities of highest standards. There is well qualified faculty in the Computer Engineering Department, which is committed to provide quality education to the students. In the Computer Engineering Department, there are three computer laboratories & one computer center with 170 computer systems with the latest configuration. All the latest softwares are installed spanning even beyond the curriculum so that the students continually upgrade their skills. All the computer laboratories & centers are well furnished & air conditioned.


Laboratories- Multimedia Lab, Networking Lab, Programming Lab, Hardware Lab, Computer Center, Digital & Microprocessor Lab.(Shared with Electronics & Communication Engg.), Basic Electrical Engg. Lab (Shared with Electronics & Communication Engg.).
