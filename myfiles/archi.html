About

An Architecture Assistantship course typically provides training and education for individuals aspiring to work as Architectural Assistants in the field of architecture. This course covers essential topics such as architectural drawing, design principles, CAD software, building materials and construction techniques. It is a great way to gain foundational knowledge and skills before pursuing a full architecture professional career or higher education.


The Department of Architecture Assistantship was started in the year 2000 at Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Govt. Polytechnic Ambota in Distt- Una (HP). We are team of experienced & innovative faculties committed to create an environment for learning which supports all developmental goals and societal needs .This three-year Architecture program has been started with an aim to provide competent professionals in the field of Architecture. Architecture department has progressed in various fields as a result of sincere dedication and devotion of its faculty as well as students. Students after completing the course are placed in reputed organizations (Govt/Private) and some are leading entrepreneurs in building industry.
