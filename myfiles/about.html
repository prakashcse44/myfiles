ABOUT OUR INSTITUTE

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar, Govt. Polytechnic Ambota was established in 1995 on Ambedkar Jyanti at village - Ambota, Tehsil - Ghanari, Distt. - Una in Himachal Pradesh.

The Polytechnic provides three years diploma courses in Computer Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering and Architecture Assistantship disciplines.

All courses are approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE).

The H.P. Takniki Shiksha Board, Dharmshala (H.P.) conducts semester examinations and Polytechnic Admission Test (PAT).

OUR VISION

We are strongly committed to providing quality technical education, at an affordable cost, especially for the downtrodden, from the backward areas fulfilling the social cause of nurturing, technically qualified employable youth, contributing towards, the building of an advanced nation.

OUR MISSION

To achieve perfection and reach the levels of excellence, in our dedicated service, of offering standard technical education and training, with improved infrastructure and human resources, to those seeking competency, for moulding their career.
